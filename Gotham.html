The Beginning

From Tragedy
To Legend

On a rain-soaked night in Gotham City, eight-year-old Bruce Wayne watched his parents, Thomas and Martha, gunned down in Crime Alley. That moment — raw, irreversible — became the fault line of a life.

Fuelled by grief and an unbreakable will, Bruce spent years training across the world: studying criminology in Europe, learning martial arts in Asia, mastering forensics and engineering at the finest institutions. He returned to Gotham not as a man — but as an idea. An idea that cannot be caged or killed.
